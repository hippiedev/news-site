I created a newspaper/article site with the aim of incorporating a retro-newspaper vibe with a mordern approach. I wanted it to be a merger of the old and new. I also aimed to achieve minimalism. Basically, something that looks simple and retro, but is efficient (like something modern). This approach led me to implement an in-article routing to the other articles (no archives page). This also influenced my decision to call it "The News"

State 3 things that have inspired you when creating your website.

  • The Wall Street Journal user interface (hence my decision for the font faces)
  • Newspapers
  • Kanye West (colors and background music)

State 3 ways in which your site is accessible.

  • Through the keyboard. By pressing the tab button, you can easily navigate to links and buttons and interact with them
  • Audio. I added audio to page 2, and page 4 includes audio-video
  • Font size adjustments for users with impaired vision

State 3 ways in which you have considered usability in your site.

  • I considered usability for users with impaired vision
  • I considered usability for users that wants to quickly run through a couple of articles by including other articles at the side
  • I considered usability for users with impaired hearing

State 3 things you had to learn/find out by yourself when creating your site, and describe how you did that

  • I realised the importance of semantic HTML in user accessibility. I initially started off with mostly div tags, when pressing the tab button to inspect accessibility through the keyboard, I noticed the div elements weren't being focused on for interactions, until I changed them to their semantic counterparts
  • I learnt that it's best not to add the "aria-role" attribute excessively. It is best to just use the proper HTML tags.
  • I learnt that the JavaScript querySelector function is most convenient for selecting HTML for DOM manipulations because its paramters are similar to css selectors

What aspect(s) of your work do you think worked well and why?

I think the font size adjustment was a nice touch.

What aspect(s) of your work could be improved, and how might you do things differently another time?

I think I can improve the user interface a bit more. I think the font I used for paragraph texts isn't so readable because it's very light. So next time, I could explore a different combination of fonts.
